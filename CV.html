41

The Design Trend Tracing & Analysis Tool

With Roland Berger, sponsored by SAIC Motor
08/2008-08/2010

The tool captures design keywords of all models and series by 16 major brands, from official medias (messages from designers) and social medias(feedbacks from users). The tool translates the keywords to a trend map monitoring global car market dynamics, analyzing trends and measuring their impacts to help designers respond accordingly.

The tool was adapted by Shanghai Automotive Group from 2008 and Volkswagen Shanghai from 2010.
