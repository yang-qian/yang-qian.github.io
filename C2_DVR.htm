China Aesthetic History For new Mazda6 Design Research

10

From 2008 to 2011, my team took on the new Mazda6 design research job to define the new Mazda6's look based on Chinese people's aesthetics.

The research includes two parts.
First part, from 2008-2009, dug into history and social stratification of modern China to give the full picture of Chinese aesthetics. Second part, from 2010-2011, traced aesthetics of Mazda target users out of the full map. The positioning was used in matching with marketing orientation and was developed into design guidelines for Mazda 6.

Stage 1 : Mapping the Modern Chinese's aesthetics
This first stage research distilled more than 50000 pictures from China Pictorial, the only publication has not been shut down during the Great Cultural Revolution (1966-1976). These pictures were clustered by time and by social class for comparison analysis.

TIME SEGMENT:The aesthetic features in different historical periods defined and demonstrated what kind of design is old, outdated or modern in China.

Stage 2 : typical users in-depth Research

The second stage tried to grasp Mazda target users' characters in the panoramic Chinese aesthetic report.
We tracked 6 typical Mazda targets, including Mazda owners and intenders, for 4 months. They were Neo-middle-class, living a young and dynamic lifestyle, among 27-30. Their aesthetic preferences were obtained through home decoration styles, car decorations and morphological analysis of their most/ least favourite artifacts. They were also invited to an eye-tracking experiments, a focus group and psychological tests and live interviews.
Control group is composed of the owners of Honda Fit, Mazda2/3’s major competitor.

Till then Mazda users’ aesthetic position was pinpointed on the China Aesthetic Map. Combing with marketing orientation and engineering intentions, the design market positioning sets design guidelines for the new Mazda6.

The final design of Mazda6 made its debut in 2013 Shanghai Auto Show.
