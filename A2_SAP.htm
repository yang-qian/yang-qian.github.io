City Experience Sharing Service

10

The award-winning idea
The idea originated from me and friends’ entry for the SAP interaction design workshop 2010. The theme of that session was Distributed Co-creation.
We first combed through manifestations of Distributed Co-creation and defined our product as a personal experience sharing platform of sounds, graphics and texts.


CITY AS YOUR MUSE: Get creative!
By introducing creative painting and sound track into the platform, we wanted the Distributed Co-creation to be a real creative process. Users can freely and creatively upload their photos, paintings or any works of visual arts involving their personal experiences/feelings of a place. They can also upload sound tracks displaying current situation of the place lively.

The website homepage displays the panoramic map of a city made up by thousands of thumbnails of pictures and sound track visualizations. Their colors indicate the atmosphere of the spots, noisy or peaceful, a place for alone time or merry carnival.

Make it real! www.LINK.com
In 2011 summer, I found several friends also passionate about this idea and started implementation. In 2011, Hong Kong Link Information Technology Co., Ltd was founded and map.linkkk.com was launched.

Compared with the competition concept, our real platform focused on both creativity and utility functions in startup phrase. Shop information and weekend schedule tool were also available.

The app: a carry-on outing advisor
We also developed the LINK app in WeChat, a multimedia messaging platform with over 300 million users in China. In the app, when one sends his location to us, hot areas and related information around him will be sent back to him.
LINK app in Apple store is currently in development.
